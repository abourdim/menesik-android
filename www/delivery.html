Shipping & Delivery
How long does standard delivery take?
Standard delivery takes 5–8 business days for international shipments. For domestic (France) it's typically 2–4 days. Delays may occur during public holidays or high-demand periods such as Hajj season.
Can I ship to Saudi Arabia for Hajj?
Yes. We offer a dedicated Hajj season delivery service to Makkah, Madinah, Jeddah, and surrounding areas. We recommend booking at least 3 weeks in advance during Dhul-Hijja. Express and overnight options are available.
What happens if no one is home at delivery?
The carrier will leave a notice and attempt delivery again the next business day. After 2 failed attempts the package is held at your local depot for 7 days. You'll receive an SMS and email notification at each attempt.
Do you offer weekend delivery?
Saturday delivery is available in major cities in France and some Middle East destinations at an additional surcharge. Sunday delivery is not available. Select Express or Overnight to see weekend availability for your route.
Tracking
When will I receive my tracking number?
Your tracking number (format: MNS-XXXX-YYYY-ZZZ) is generated immediately when you submit your order. It's shown on screen and sent to your email. Tracking becomes active once the package is collected.
My tracking hasn't updated in 2 days — is that normal?
Tracking gaps of 24–48h are common during international transit, especially when passing through customs. If there is no update after 72 hours, please contact our support team with your tracking number.
Pricing & Payment
How is the shipping cost calculated?
Cost is based on the greater of actual weight vs volumetric weight (L × W × H ÷ 5000). The destination zone, service level (Standard/Express/Overnight), and any declared value for insurance also affect the final price. You'll see an estimate before confirming.
Are there customs duties or taxes?
Import duties depend on the destination country and the declared value of the goods. For Saudi Arabia, items under SAR 1,000 are typically duty-free. We provide all required customs documentation, but the recipient is responsible for any applicable duties.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept Visa / Mastercard, bank transfer (IBAN), and cash on collection. PayPal is available for orders under €500. Payment is collected at the time of pickup or drop-off unless you have a business account with monthly invoicing.
Claims & Support
What if my package is lost or damaged?
Open a claim within 14 days of the delivery date (or expected date for lost packages). Attach photos of damage and your tracking number. Basic coverage reimburses up to €150. Premium insurance covers up to €2,000. Claims are processed within 5–10 business days.
Can I cancel or modify my order?
You can cancel or modify your order free of charge up to 1 hour before the scheduled pickup. After pickup, address changes may incur a €5 redirection fee. Contact support immediately with your tracking number.
